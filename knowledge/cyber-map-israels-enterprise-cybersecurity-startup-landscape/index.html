The Israeli Enterprise Cybersecurity Startup Landscape

Prepared by Avery E. Schwartz, Partner

If you would like to add your company to the map, please reach out to David at david@greenfield-growth.com

We’re proud to share our Cyber Map of Israel’s enterprise Cybersecurity startup landscape.

As active investors within the Israeli cyber ecosystem, we're excited to highlight the breadth and depth of the local landscape - from emerging startups to global leaders - all innovating at the forefront of cybersecurity.

Amidst times like these of market volatility, we expect cybersecurity to remain a key spending priority for enterprises, and the local ecosystem to continue moving forward.  

Additionally, we're proud to highlight our own cyber portfolio with Cynet operating within XDR, Panorays in third-party risk, Silverfort in identity detection and response, Torq in SOAR, and Coralogix’s Snowbit in SIEM, all leading the charge within their respective domains.
