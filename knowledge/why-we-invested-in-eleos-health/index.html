Empowering Behavioral Health Through AI: Why We Invested in Eleos Health

Itay InbarPartner
Nir GoldsteinOperating Partner

The Silent Crisis: A Growing Gap in Behavioral Health

The demand for mental health services is soaring, yet the supply of clinicians is rapidly dwindling, creating a crisis that could leave a 4.5 million clinician gap by 2030. Mental health professionals, already stretched thin, are drowning in administrative tasks, with only half their time devoted to patient care. Outdated Electronic Health Records (EHRs) compound the problem, adding burdensome workflows that contribute to clinician burnout and turnover rates as high as 40%. In some cases, a simple 5-minute call can require 25 minutes of documentation, highlighting the urgent need for smarter solutions.

This inefficiency has left clinicians overwhelmed and unable to focus on patient care, underscoring the importance of finding solutions that not only alleviate administrative burdens, but also ensure that patients receive the focused, quality care they deserve.

The Untapped Opportunity: Enter Eleos

While AI innovation in acute care has driven fierce competition, behavioral health has largely been overlooked. Behavioral health has unique workflows and technological needs that differ significantly from those of acute care, leaving incumbent EHR providers struggling to adapt their AI functionality to this specialized field.

Eleos is poised to change this landscape, harnessing cutting-edge generative AI to transform behavioral health conversations into automated scribing and actionable insights that drive better care, efficiency, and decision-making across the clinical workflow.

Eleos is setting a new standard in the industry with deep, behavioral health-specific integrations workflows, and AI models fine-tuned for the nuances of mental healthcare. With more than 120 customer organizations across over 30 states, Eleos is the most-deployed AI platform in behavioral health today. Their solution has already proven its worth, reducing documentation times by over 50% and driving significant efficiency gains for clinicians while allowing them to focus on what matters most—patient care.

More Than a Tool: Eleos as an Essential Partner

Eleos goes beyond just offering a solution—it’s embedding itself into the heart of behavioral health care. Positioned as a central AI platform between providers and EHRs, Eleos uses a simple browser extension to interface with a proprietary AI core, automating cumbersome EHR workflows and turning complex documentation into a streamlined process. Fast implementation times of just 14-30 days ensure that clinics can quickly benefit from the platform’s capabilities, making Eleos not just a tool, but a vital partner in improving clinician productivity and retention.

A Partnership Rooted in Vision and Execution

We’ve been tracking Eleos since their Series B, drawn to the vision and leadership of CEO Alon Joffe and Co-Founders Dror Zaide (CRO) and Alon Rabinovich (CTO). Together, they have built an exceptional team and a phenomenal culture that prioritizes innovation, collaboration, and execution. This culture has driven not only strong product innovation but also a level of go-to-market maturity rarely seen at this stage. Conversations with Eleos customers consistently highlight high satisfaction and a clear recognition of the critical need for automated documentation processes—needs that Eleos addresses with precision and adaptability.

Ready to Scale: Eleos’ Next Big Leap

Eleos is on a trajectory to become the go-to AI leader in behavioral health, seizing a massive, untapped opportunity with a platform that's redefining how care is delivered, managed, and documented. With strong execution to date, Eleos stands at a pivotal point in scaling its go-to-market efforts and expanding into new products and markets. Greenfield’s expertise in go-to-market strategies and hands-on approach position us to play a meaningful role in supporting Eleos as they navigate this next phase of growth.

“We're proud to back the phenomenal team at Eleos Health as they revolutionize the behavioral health space - a market of immense importance and scale which is facing severe challenges.

Their transformative product alleviates the overwhelming administrative burden faces by providers, empowering them to focus on delivering exceptional care. Eleos Health's innovative use of AI is reshaping the way behavioral health care is provided, and we are excited to be part of their journey.”

- Shay Grinfeld, Managing Partner

Our commitment is further strengthened by a board that brings deep strategic and healthcare expertise, alongside the support of stellar investors participating in this Series C funding round. Led by Greenfield Partners, the round includes existing investors such as F-Prime Capital, Eight Roads, Menlo Ventures, and ION, as well as new partners including the Michael & Susan Dell Foundation, Union Tech Venture, and Centerstone, all aligning perfectly with Eleos’ growth trajectory.

Looking Ahead: Scaling New Heights Together

As Eleos continues to refine its platform and expand its reach, we are committed to working closely with them to navigate the complexities of scaling. We see a future where Eleos not only dominates the behavioral health market but also sets new benchmarks for what AI can achieve in healthcare. By continuously leveraging customer data and feedback, Eleos is creating a flywheel effect that propels their model accuracy and market momentum. Our journey with Eleos is not just about investment; it’s about partnering to shape the future of behavioral health, driving real, tangible improvements in care delivery.
