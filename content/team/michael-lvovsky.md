Michael is the Financial Controller at Greenfield, with broad experience in finance and law. Previously, he was the controller at Hanaco Ventures, managing financial operations for early- and growth-stage funds. He served as a Manager in the International Tax Department at Ernst & Young, specializing in multinational tax strategies, and practiced corporate and commercial law at Nashitz Brandes Amir.

Michael holds a BA in Business Management (Accounting) and an LLB in Law from the College of Management. He is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and a licensed lawyer.
