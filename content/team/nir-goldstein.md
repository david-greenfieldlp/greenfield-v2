Nir joined Greenfield after serving as VP Sales EMEA & APAC at monday.com where he led a team of 180 professionals.

During his tenure at monday.com, Nir played a pivotal role during the company's growth period and subsequent IPO, founded the monday.com High-Tech Sales Academy, and actively participates in the governmental initiative "People for High-Tech." Before that, Nir was the sales director at Sisense.

Nir oversees Greenfield's initiatives with portfolio companies to catalyze their growth and enhance their global sales expansion, as well as identifying new investments.

Nir holds a bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering and Management with honors from Tel Aviv University.
