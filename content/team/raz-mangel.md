Raz joined Greenfield following a few years in investment banking at Barclays in Israel where he focused mostly on software, semiconductor, financial services and healthcare companies. He advised clients on a wide variety of investment banking transactions, including mergers and acquisitions, debt and equity capital markets transactions, and credit facilities.

Prior to Barclays, Raz worked at EY's Transaction Advisory team, as a Management Consultant at Tefen, and served as a Captain in the IDF.

Raz holds a Bachelor's Degree, Double Major in Accounting and Economics from Tel Aviv University, and an M.B.A. from the University of Chicago, Booth School of Business.
