Before joining Greenfield, Josh was an investor at Nauta Capital in London, where he focused on cybersecurity, infrastructure, and enterprise software investments across Europe. Prior to his time at Nauta, Josh was in the M&A team at Strategy& PwC, specializing in large-cap private equity buyouts.

He also helped build Arthur, a PropTech platform, as its first product manager, which was later acquired by Advent International. Josh holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Edinburgh.
