Shay is the Co-Founder and Managing Partner at Greenfield Partners, focusing on SaaS, healthcare and AI. He leads Greenfield's investments in BigPanda, Avanan, Coralogix, Capitolis, Mixtiles, and Quali. Prior to Founding Greenfield, Shay was a senior advisor to TPG Growth.

Originally from Tel Aviv and for the last 25 years in NYC, Shay spent the early years of his career operating and maturing early growth technology companies. He then joined Thylacine Capital to lead the alternative investing activity.

Shay has an undergraduate degree from Fairleigh Dickenson University and an EMBA from Columbia Business School.
