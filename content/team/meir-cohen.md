Meir joined Greenfield following experience as an Investment Analyst at TFRG Capital, a real estate investment fund, where he helped source deals and model investments across the US and Israel. Prior to TFRG, Meir worked in Business Development and previously served as a Staff Sergeant in the IDF Paratroopers Brigade.

Meir holds a Bachelor's Degree in Finance & Business Administration from IDC Herzliya (Magna Cum Laude).
