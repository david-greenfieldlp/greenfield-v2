Prior to joining Greenfield as Operations Manager for our New York office, Yael was the Operations Lead at Rally, an alternative investment platform for collectibles, where she managed valuations and day to day ops. Before that, she worked as an analyst at Massarsky Consulting, specializing in music catalog valuations.

Yael holds a Master's of Science in Industrial/Organizational Psychology from Baruch College and a Bachelor's in psychology from Binghamton University.
