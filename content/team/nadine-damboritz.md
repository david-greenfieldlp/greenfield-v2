Nadine manages the day to day operations of Greenfield's Tel Aviv offices and concurrently serves as Executive Assistant to managing partner Shay Grinfeld. Prior to joining the Greenfield team, Nadine was Head of Operations at Kahana, a Jerusalem-based digital marketing firm, and previously worked in various capacities with non-profits and startups in Israel, Europe, and the US.

Nadine is a graduate of the Sy Syms School of Business at Yeshiva University in New York, where she received her BS in Marketing. She is a proud mother of four and enjoys reading and cooking in her free time.
