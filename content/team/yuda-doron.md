Yuda is a prolific angel investor in Israeli high-tech, having backed 40+ companies over the last decade including Appilog (acq. by Mercury), Bristol (acq. by HP), PrimeSense (Apple), Crosswise (Oracle), Appsee (ServiceNow), and Leaba (Cisco). Yuda serves as Senior Advisor to TPG, and previously served as Senior Advisor to Silver Lake Sumeru.

Prior to his investing career, Yuda managed the domestic sales channel at Computer Associates and held prominent positions at Cheyenne Software and Texas Instruments. He is also founder of Texel (acq. by Elco International).
