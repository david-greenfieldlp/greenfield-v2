## Why We're Launching Greenfield Knowledge

At Greenfield Partners, we've spent years working alongside exceptional technology companies at the early growth stage. Through this work, we've accumulated insights about what separates companies that scale sustainably from those that stall.

**Greenfield Knowledge** is where we share those insights with the broader ecosystem — founders, operators, and fellow investors.

### What to Expect

We'll be publishing regularly on topics including:

- **Growth-stage investing** — frameworks we use to evaluate opportunities at the inflection point between product-market fit and scale
- **Go-to-market strategy** — lessons from our G²M platform on building repeatable sales motions
- **Technology trends** — deep dives into AI infrastructure, cybersecurity, defense tech, and other sectors where we invest
- **Founder stories** — conversations with portfolio company leaders about the challenges and breakthroughs of scaling

### Our Perspective

We believe the best growth-stage companies share three qualities:

1. A product that solves a critical problem in a large market
2. Early evidence of repeatable customer acquisition
3. A founding team with the ambition and adaptability to scale

Our role as investors is to accelerate the path from early traction to market leadership — and this blog is an extension of that mission.

> "The best time to share knowledge is before people need it. The second best time is now."

Stay tuned for our first deep-dive piece, coming soon.
